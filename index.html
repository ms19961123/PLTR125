Financial Planning & Analysis

+

How FP&A Drives Vanguard Strategy, Performance, and Accountability

+

+ Vanguard FP&A connects enterprise strategy to day-to-day decisions through long-range planning, + annual budgeting, rolling forecasts, capital allocation, and executive performance insights. + This landing page summarizes the end-to-end operating model used to guide growth, control risk, + and sustain client value. +

+
+
+ +
+
+

1) Strategic & Annual Planning

+
+
+

Long-Range Plan (LRP)

+

Translates Vanguard's multi-year objectives into revenue, expense, headcount, and investment targets by business line.

+
+
+

Annual Operating Plan

+

Builds a one-year budget with quarterly phasing, aligned to strategic priorities, cost discipline, and capacity assumptions.

+
+
+

Resource Prioritization

+

Ranks initiatives by client impact, expected returns, regulatory needs, and execution risk to ensure disciplined spend.

+
+
+

Scenario Design

+

Assesses market and business uncertainty through base, upside, and downside scenarios to improve resilience.

+
+
+
+
+ +
+
+

2) Forecasting & Driver-Based Insights

+
+
+

Rolling Forecasts

+

Monthly or quarterly reforecasts update full-year outlooks for assets, flows, margins, and expense trajectories.

+
+
+

Key Business Drivers

+

Forecasts are anchored on actionable drivers such as market levels, client behavior, pricing, staffing, and productivity.

+
+
+

Variance Intelligence

+

FP&A explains plan-versus-actual and forecast-versus-plan variances with clear root causes and actions.

+
+
+

3) Performance Management & Executive Reporting

+
+
+

KPI Framework

+

Tracks financial and operating indicators across growth, efficiency, service quality, and risk outcomes.

+
+
+

Leadership Reviews

+

Produces concise monthly business reviews and board-ready materials with trends, implications, and decisions required.

+
+
+

Action Tracking

+

Monitors commitments from review forums and quantifies expected impact to maintain execution accountability.

+
+
+

Benchmarking

+

Compares key metrics versus internal targets and external peers to support continuous performance improvement.

4) Governance, Risk, and Capital Discipline

+
+
+

Investment Governance

+

Applies stage-gate and business-case standards to evaluate strategic programs and technology investments.

+
+
+

Controls & Compliance

+

Maintains robust financial controls, transparent assumptions, and clear audit trails across planning cycles.

+
+
+

Balance of Growth & Cost

+

Supports decisions that protect long-term client value while managing near-term operating leverage.
